Ceviche

Ceviche is a seafood dish that is popular in Latin America. It is made by marinating raw fish in citrus juices, such as lemon or lime. The fish is then cooked by the acid in the juice.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. of fish (cod, halibut, or tilapia)
  • 1/2 cup of lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup of lime juice
  • 1/2 cup of orange juice
  • 1/2 cup of onion
  • 1/2 cup of cilantro
  • 1/2 cup of tomato
  • 1/2 cup of salt
  • 1/2 cup of pepper
