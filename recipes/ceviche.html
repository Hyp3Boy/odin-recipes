Description

Ceviche is a seafood dish that is popular in Latin America. It is made by marinating raw fish in citrus juices, such as lemon or lime.
The fish is then cooked by the acid in the juice. Also is usually served as an appetizer or as a side dish. It is often served with tortilla chips or crackers.
Ceviche is a popular dish in Peru, where it is often served with chicha.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. of fish (cod, halibut, or tilapia)
  • 1/2 cup of lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup of lime juice
  • 1/2 cup of orange juice
  • 1/2 cup of onion
  • 1/2 cup of cilantro
  • 1/2 cup of tomato
  • 1/2 cup of salt
  • 1/2 cup of pepper

Instructions

  1. Cut the fish into small pieces.
  2. Put the fish in a bowl.
  3. Add the lemon, lime, and orange juice.
  4. Add the onion, cilantro, tomato, salt, and pepper.
  5. Let the fish marinate for 30 minutes.
  6. Enjoy!
